ideal working style

  • direct communication - i treat everything as a growth opportunity
  • living together - you are who you surround yourself by
  • obsessed and passionate - i need to be obsessed and overcome with passion. almost everything i make literally overwhelms me with emotion.
  • aesthetically oriented - i have a very strong imagination and creative eye. i like iterating on things until they look extremely close to how they do in my head. i see issues down to the pixel. i am always aware of every imperfection.
  • larger ambiguous scope - i do my best work when i'm thrown in some direction and allowed to work on whatever i spot in that direction. i often pick up several tasks at the same time depending on what distracts me. this (working on one big thing haphazardly) is more natural to me than working on one small thing at a time from a list.
